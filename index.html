About the Barangay

Serving Calabayan
with Pride

Barangay Calabayan is dedicated to delivering efficient public service, maintaining peace and order, and supporting sustainable community development through digital transparency and active resident engagement.

Located in Ozamiz City, Misamis Occidental, Calabayan continues to grow as a model barangay in Northern Mindanao — where every voice counts and every project serves the people.
